Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name:Traditional Gaucho Center "El Rodeo" of Magdalena

  • This name clearly identifies the organization and its location. "El Rodeo" adds a traditional and authentic touch that evokes gaucho activities.
  • Optional Domain Availability: centro-elrodeo.org.ar

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official portal for the **Centro Gaucho Tradicional Magdalena**, with the following purposes:

  • **Preservation and Promotion:** To showcase the history, values, and activities of gaucho culture in the Magdalena region of Buenos Aires Province.
  • **Community Information:** To provide details about the Center's history, events, photo gallery, and contact information to encourage local participation.
  • **Tourist Attraction:** To serve as a cultural point of interest for visitors, offering a window into the rich gaucho traditions.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent common questions a visitor might have that the website should answer:

  • **Scenario 1 (Local Visitor):** "Where can I see photos from the Center's latest events, and how do I sign up for the next gaucho festival?"
  • **Scenario 2 (Tourist):** "What does it mean to be a gaucho, and what is the history of the Gaucho Center in Magdalena? Where is it located so I can visit?"
  • **Scenario 3 (Interested in Collaborating):** "I'd like to contact the organizers to offer my help with activities or make a donation. What's the best way to get in touch?"

Color Scheme

The color scheme is inspired by the earthy tones of the Pampa and traditional materials, creating a sense of warmth and authenticity.

  • **Earthy Brown (#8B4513):** For titles, headers, and important elements, symbolizing the land, leather, and tradition.
  • **Soft Beige (#D2B48C):** As an accent color and for section backgrounds, representing the color of dry grass or aged wood.
  • **Dark Gray (#4A4A4A):** For body text, ensuring excellent readability on light backgrounds.
  • **Light Cream (#F8F4E1):** As the main background color for the site, evoking parchment paper or light earth, creating a welcoming atmosphere.

Typography

The font selection aims to balance traditional elegance with modern clarity, reflecting the Center's character.

  • Heading Font: A classic serif font like **'Palatino Linotype'** or **'Georgia'** will be used. These fonts convey a sense of seriousness, elegance, and history, perfect for titles like "History" or "Gallery".
  • Body Text Font: A readable serif font like **'Georgia'** will be chosen for paragraphs, offering comfortable reading and a traditional style.
